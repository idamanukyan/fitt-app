# HyperFit Design System
## Minimal Futuristic Neon-Brutalist Interface

**Version 1.0** | Last Updated: November 2025

---

## Design Philosophy

HyperFit embodies a **minimal, futuristic, brutalist aesthetic with neon cyberpunk accents**.

The design evokes an AI-powered fitness lab—modern health-tech meets Tron-inspired brutalism.

### Core Principles

1. **Brutalist Foundation**: Sharp rectangles, matte dark surfaces
2. **Neon Accents**: Strategic glowing edges and highlights
3. **Technical Precision**: Tight letter-spacing, uppercase headings
4. **Dark Depth**: Rich blacks with subtle gradients
5. **Modular Clarity**: Components feel like panels in a fitness OS

---

## Color System

### Primary Palette
- Rich Black: `#000F0B` — Main background
- Mountain Meadow: `#2CC295` — Primary neon accent
- Caribbean Green: `#00BF81` — Secondary neon accent
- Anti-Flash White: `#F7F7F6` — Primary text

### Usage
- Backgrounds: Rich Black + Dark Green
- Cards: Bangladesh Green with Mountain Meadow borders
- Text: Anti-Flash White (primary), Stone (secondary)
- Accents: Mountain Meadow for glows and highlights

---

## Typography

**Font**: AxiForma (Regular, Medium, Semi Bold, Bold)

### Type Scale
- 5xl (36px): Page titles
- 4xl (32px): Section headers
- md (16px): Default body
- xs (12px): Captions, labels

### Letter Spacing
- Ultra (4px): Page titles
- Wide (2px): Labels
- Normal (0px): Body text
